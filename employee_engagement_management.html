EMPLOYEE ENGAGEMENT MANAGEMENT

Employee engagement is a key concept in the workplace that is concerned with the extent of relationship and commitment between an organization and its employees. It is identified to be one of the major forces behind successful organizations in today’s competitive business world. Recently, top organizations have admitted the adoption of employee engagement among their top business strategies. This is concerned with the fact that employee engagement is directly related to the success of any organization. Apart from possessing the potency to significantly influence employee retention, productivity and loyalty, it also has a strong connection with customer satisfaction and the reputation of an organization.
